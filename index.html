Research Interests

I have a particular interest in applying mathematics and logic in software engineering. To be more specific, it is application of formal specification and verification in safety critical systems and cyber physical systems (CPS). My interests lie in several aspects:

  • formal specification languages (such as Circus and CML) to model state-rich reactive and concurrent systems,
  • denotational and operational semantics of formal languages by UTP,
  • formal verification by model checking, and related tools development.

PhD Thesis

My PhD research is a reflection of my interests. It is an integrated solution, including theoretical definitions, semantics-based soundness, tools, and industrial case studies, to model-check the state-rich formal language Circus.

Title: Model Checking of State-Rich Formalisms - By Linking to Combination of State-based Formalism and Process Algebra

My submitted version is avaiable here (pdf).

The objective of my PhD research is to study and develop a model checking solution for Circus (Concurrent Integrated Refinement CalculUS) which is a state-rich formal language capable of specifying systems from both state and behavioural aspects. The advantages of my approach are manifold:

  • high-level abstraction preservation
  • partial support of looseness
  • support of nondeterminism
  • refinement checking
  • basic deadlock and divergence checking
  • temporal logic checking
  • animation, and debug with counterexamples.

The contributions of my study consist of several parts:

  • Defined a formal link from Circus to a combination of CSP and B (CSP || B)
  • Proved soundness of the link based on their semantics in UTP

  • A collection of CSP libraries that implement expressions, operators, and predicates in Circus (or ISO Z), for example

    • the partial function implementation in CSP by pfun
    • the finite sequence (seq) implementation in CSP by fseq
    • the schema as type support in CSP by extending the ProB kernel

  • Developed a translator Circus2ZCSP in Java to link Circus models to CSP || B models automatically

  • Completed three case studies: a bounded reactive buffer, an Electronic Shelf Edge Label (ESEL) system, and the steam boiler control system
  • Made/implemented a number of improvements/features in the kernel of ProB (SICStus Prolog)
